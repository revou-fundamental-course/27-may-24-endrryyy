Company Profile

30 TransPlesiran was established in 2012, we are from Blora, Central Java and we are a transportation company that serves passengers to many destinations in Java, Bali, and Sumatra Islands. 30 Transplesiran services include overnight buses, charter buses, logistics, and holiday packages. Transportation is crucial as it supports the country's development.
